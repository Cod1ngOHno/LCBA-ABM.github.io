What is "ABM" first?

ABM, which stands for Accountancy, Business, and Management, is one of LCBA's Senior High School (SHS) programs offered under the Academic Track.

The Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) Strand equips students with essential knowledge and skills in business, finance, and entrepreneurship. It helps learners develop strong leadership, decision-making, and management abilities, preparing them for future careers in accounting, business administration, marketing, banking, and other business-related fields.

This strand is best for students who are interested in:

  • Business and Entrepreneurship
  • Accounting and Finance
  • Marketing and Management
  • Economics and Corporate Operations

What You Learn in ABM

In ABM, you will study subjects like:

  • Fundamentals of Accounting, Business, and Management
  • Business Mathematics
  • Applied Economics
  • Principles of Marketing
  • Business Ethics and Social Responsibility
  • Entrepreneurship

Possible College Courses After ABM

ABM is a great foundation if you plan to take:

  • Accountancy
  • Business Administration
  • Financial Management
  • Marketing Management
  • Entrepreneurship
  • Economics

Future Careers

ABM students often pursue careers such as:

  • Accountant
  • Business owner / Entrepreneur
  • Marketing Officer
  • Bank or Finance Employee
  • Manager or Corporate Professional
